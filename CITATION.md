## Key dependencies to cite

If you use the simulation, please also cite the physiological literature the parameters are drawn from:

- **Banister fatigue model** (Eq. 3): Banister et al. (1975). A systems model of training for athletic performance. *Australian Journal of Sports Medicine*, 7(3), 57–61.
- **HRV standards** (Eq. 5): Task Force of the European Society of Cardiology (1996). Heart rate variability: standards of measurement. *Circulation*, 93(5), 1043–1065.
- **GPS validation** (Eq. 7): Scott et al. (2016). The validity and reliability of GPS in team sport. *Journal of Strength and Conditioning Research*, 30(5), 1470–1490.
- **EDA decomposition** (Eq. 8): Benedek & Kaernbach (2010). Decomposition of skin conductance data. *Journal of Neuroscience Methods*, 190(1), 80–91.
